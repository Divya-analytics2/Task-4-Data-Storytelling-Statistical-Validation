# Hypothesis Testing Summary – One-Way ANOVA

## Objective
To determine whether there is a statistically significant difference in sales among West, East, Central, and South regions.

## Hypotheses
H₀: μ₁ = μ₂ = μ₃ = μ₄ (No significant difference in regional sales)  
H₁: At least one regional mean is different

## Significance Level
α = 0.05

## Results
F = 0.8974  
P-value = 0.4416  
F-critical = 2.6058  

## Interpretation
Since the P-value is greater than the significance level (0.05), we fail to reject the null hypothesis.

## Final Conclusion
Sales differences among regions are not statistically significant.
